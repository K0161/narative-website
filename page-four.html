About The Life Guide, Manchester


My name is Karan, the creator of this website, I am a full-time student currently at Manchester Metropolitan University studying Web and UX design. I had noticed that there isn’t much information about student living in Manchester, my goal was to help students become comfortable and independent with moving to university.

Since I am a student within Manchester and have been living here near enough my whole life, I am a reputable and trustworthy source for this type of information.

This website was created to help students find the best locations for food, bars and clubs around the Manchester area without breaking the bank. The locations have been carefully selected to fit everyone’s needs, from cheap and fun locations to places which are more expensive with a more professional atmosphere.

You can get into contact with me to find out more information via email (karan1313@outlook.com) or phone (07808028219).
