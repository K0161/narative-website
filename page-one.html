Clubs and Nightlife

Vision Nightclub

Vision, formerly known as 'Playground', has opened it's doors recently bringing a new wave of enjoyment for your next few weekends. With the club being located within walking distance from Oxford Road train station and just a few minutes away from Deansgate, this club is definitely one to add to your next weekends adventures.

Revolution Deansgate

Enjoy artisan beverages on their outdoor sun deck before heading downstairs to a crowded dance floor until late. We can assure you that at Revolution Deansgate Locks, the excitement never ends. If you were seeking student filled excitment, Revolution Deansgate is the place to be, as it frequently hosts Manchester's best nights out. You won't want to leave when the night is young thanks to their amazing drink selection and nonstop music . Gather your group of friends, shine your dancing shoes, and get ready for one of the best nights of your lives.

Featured Club Of The Month

Ark Deansgate

With events running from nearly every day of the week, Ark Deansgate is at the top of our list for this month. With having 3 different floors of non-stop bangers, there is a guarantee that people with a wide range of music tastes will have an entertaining night. Ark is located within the Deansgate locks which is a close destination for students, being just a 10 minute walk from most accommodations.
