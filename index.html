Clubs

A brand new nightclub in the heart of Manchester!

Vision, formerly known as 'Playground', has opened it's doors recently bringing a new wave of enjoyment for your next few weekends. With the club being located within walking distance from Oxford Road train station and just a few minutes away from Deansgate, this club is definetly one to add to yout next weekends adventures. The music in the club ranges from DnB events, house, rap, rnb and much more so whatever music your into, Vision will provide a solid night of entertainment.
